Discover exciting destinations in your neighbourhood and on the other side of the globe.

We are local

City Explorer helps you find exciting places that you can walk to. You'll see pictures, a short history, distance, and directions.

As you move around, the application adapts to your location, presenting you with a personalized selection of pages.

We are global

The City Explorer app is available worldwide, making it just as simple to begin exploring exotic locations as it is in your neighbourhood.

Remember, you have to be at a location before you can start exploring it through your app.
